Choose Your Own Adventure

A step-by-step companion that guides you through three agents: Research → Design → Charter. Keep your outputs from each step — you’ll paste them into the next one.

How to use this companion

Follow the sections in order. Each one produces an output you’ll reuse.

Workshop flow (3 agents)

1) Research Agent → 2) Design Agent → 3) Charter Agent

Next: we’ll add “copy prompt” blocks and a place to paste/save outputs.
